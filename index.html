About FNRP

Fargo North Dakota Roleplay

A realistic, community-driven ER:LC roleplay server built on structure, immersion, and growth.

What is FNRP?

Fargo North Dakota Roleplay (FNRP) is an Emergency Response: Liberty City (ER:LC) private Roblox roleplay community. We operate a fully structured server inspired by the real city of Fargo, North Dakota.

Unlike casual public servers, FNRP is organized into real departments with genuine rank structures, protocols, and chain-of-command systems. Every member has a role. Every session has purpose.

Our Mission

To create the most immersive, structured, and welcoming ER:LC roleplay experience on Roblox. We believe great roleplay comes from great community — and great community is built through structure, respect, and shared passion.

Our Vision

FNRP aims to become one of the premier ER:LC communities on Roblox — expanding our departments, hosting regular sessions, and growing a dedicated, friendly playerbase.

  • Realistic department operations modeled after real emergency services
  • Regular structured roleplay sessions with all departments active
  • A welcoming environment for both new and experienced roleplayers
  • Continuous growth and improvement based on community feedback
  • A transparent, fair leadership structure everyone can trust

Community Values

Respect, professionalism, and immersion are the three pillars of FNRP. We hold ourselves to a high standard — not because we have to, but because we care about the experience we create for each other.

Help & Info

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FNRP.

General
What is ER:LC / ERLC?▼

Emergency Response: Liberty City (ER:LC) is a popular Roblox game where players roleplay as police officers, firefighters, EMS, or civilians in a city environment.

What is FNRP?▼

FNRP (Fargo North Dakota Roleplay) is a structured ER:LC community with organized departments, real rank systems, and scheduled sessions operating a private server.

Is FNRP free to join?▼

Yes! Joining FNRP is completely free. You'll need a Roblox account and the ER:LC game.

Departments
How do I join a department?▼

Join the main FNRP Discord, then join the specific department Discord. Each department has an application channel. You'll be contacted for an interview by department command.

Can I be in multiple departments?▼

You may hold a civilian role alongside one primary department. Staff members may have special cross-department access depending on their rank.

What departments are currently open?▼

FPD, FFD, and DOT are open for applications. RDD is still in development. Check the Departments page for the latest status.

Sessions & Roleplay
How do sessions work?▼

Sessions are scheduled events where all departments go in-game together for structured roleplay. Session times are announced in the main Discord.

Are microphones (VC) required?▼

A working microphone is strongly recommended, especially for department members during sessions. Dispatch may require voice communication.

Staff & Applications
How do I apply to be a staff member?▼

Use the Applications page on this website to submit a staff application. We look for active, mature, and helpful community members.

How long do applications take to review?▼

We aim to review all applications within 48 hours. Ensure your Discord DMs are open so we can contact you.
